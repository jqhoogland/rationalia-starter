# Lightcone Infrastructure

[**Lightcone Infrastructure**](https://www.lightconeinfrastructure.com/) is the parent organization of LessWrong. Its mission is to build infrastructure for people who are helping the [[EA/Topics/Longtermism|long term]] future of humanity. It was [announced](https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/eR7Su77N2nK3e5YRZ/) on October 1st, 2021.
