# Intelligence explosion

An **intelligence explosion** (sometimes called a **technological singularity**, or **singularity** for short) is a hypothesized event in which a sufficiently advanced [[Artificial intelligence|artificial intelligence]] rapidly attains [[Superintelligence|superhuman intellectual ability]] by a process of recursive self-improvement.
