# Alignment Newsletter

The **Alignment Newsletter** provides weekly summaries and opinions on new research relevant to [[AI alignment]]. It was created in 2018 by Rohin Shah (then a PhD student at Berkeley, currently a researcher at [[DeepMind]]) who continues to run it and writes most of the content.^[\[1\]](#fn7a6uaiohgog)^Â As of May 2022, the newsletter has over 2600 subscribers.
