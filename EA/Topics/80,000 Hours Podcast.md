# 80,000 Hours Podcast

The **80,000 Hours Podcast** is a podcast by [[80,000 Hours]]. It is primarily hosted by Robert Wiblin, and produced by Keiran Harris. The first episode was released in June 2017.

Past guests of the show include [[Sam Bankman-Fried]],^[\[1\]](#fnu4n2oq2zos)^ [[Nick Beckstead]],^[\[2\]](#fn47grgpwls3a)^ [[Alexander Berger]],^[\[3\]](#fnh2rvh97gc7m)^ [[Vitalik Buterin]],^[\[4\]](#fnjmcom6yrm9)^ [[Bryan Caplan]],^[\[5\]](#fnaas1k198r6b)^^[\[6\]](#fnzfa7ikfg4hr)^ [[David Chalmers]],^[\[7\]](#fndaaglpuve5d)^ [[Paul Christiano]],^[\[8\]](#fnpznus671oqa)^^[\[9\]](#fnrje144nn4wf)^ [[Tyler Cowen]],^[\[10\]](#fnwq9zh8ijm7)^ [[Julia Galef]],^[\[11\]](#fnjqfh428ej8c)^ [[Hilary Greaves]],^[\[12\]](#fn42bl0mefhmm)^^[\[13\]](#fnz5bh1uthvg)^ [[Robin Hanson]],^[\[14\]](#fn5r7y21upzs)^ [[Holden Karnofsky]],^[\[15\]](#fn0qfakaubr39n)^^[\[16\]](#fn85g39pz2zn7)^^[\[17\]](#fnwzwd4pgcvyg)^ [[William MacAskill]],^[\[18\]](#fng9ufe23imbc)^^[\[19\]](#fn7vgxyoiqc5b)^ [[Yew-Kwang Ng]],^[\[20\]](#fnedpebti7j0i)^ [[Toby Ord]],^[\[21\]](#fnaqjlz06wy2q)^^[\[22\]](#fnz13my57t13)^ [[Kelsey Piper]],^[\[23\]](#fn9lt3pkkpiho)^ [[Stuart Russell]],^[\[24\]](#fn1xwrr7k8yip)^ [[Anders Sandberg]],^[\[25\]](#fnqm83tqaoy5)^^[\[26\]](#fny22o0tbpya)^ [[Carl Shulman]],^[\[27\]](#fnuvlfsgmbmdf)^ [[Peter Singer]],^[\[28\]](#fn6xiibvhm95d)^ [[Philip Tetlock]]^[\[29\]](#fnsnqduqbsfp)^^[\[30\]](#fn7goxys84qn8)^ and many other notable figures in the effective altruism community or [[Communities adjacent to effective altruism|adjacent to it]].
