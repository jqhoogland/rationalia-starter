# Future of Humanity Institute

The **Future of Humanity Institute** (**FHI**) is a multidisciplinary research institute at the University of Oxford studying big picture questions for human civilization. It was founded in 2005 by [[Nick Bostrom]], who is also its current Director.

Funding
-------

As of July 2022, FHI has received over $20 million in grants from [[Open Philanthropy]],^[\[1\]](#fn8w1z26v038t)^ and nearly $250,000 the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund.^[\[2\]](#fnb1x6y65zvu7)^
