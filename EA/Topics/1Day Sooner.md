# 1Day Sooner

**1Day Sooner** is a nonprofit organization that advocates on behalf of volunteers willing to participate in high-risk, high-impact medical research.

History
-------

1Day Sooner was founded in April 2020 to promote the use of [[Human challenge trials|human challenge trials]] as a way to speed up the development of [[COVID-19 pandemic|COVID-19]] vaccines. The organization has now expanded to work on other types of challenge trials, as well as other projects such as [[Pandemic preparedness|pandemic preparedness]] and [[Vaccines|vaccine equity]].

Funding 
--------

As of July 2022, 1Day Sooner has received a total of $3.1 million in grants from [[Open Philanthropy]],^[\[1\]](#fn0u9dmz0thj6f)^ $500,000 from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund,^[\[2\]](#fn5zbl7ccyr5t)^ and $350,000 from the [[Future Fund]].^[\[3\]](#fn9n48hu87rb)^
