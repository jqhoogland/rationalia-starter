# Effective Thesis

**Effective Thesis** is a project to help university students find high-impact thesis topics.

As of July 2022, Effective Thesis has advised or is currently advising over 40 different students writing theses in areas such as [[Global priorities research|global priorities research]], [[Animal welfare|animal welfare]], [[Biosecurity|biosecurity]], and [[AI safety]].
