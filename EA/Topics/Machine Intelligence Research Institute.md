# Machine Intelligence Research Institute

The **Machine Intelligence Research Institute** (**MIRI**) is a non-profit research institute. Its mission is "to develop formal tools for the clean design and analysis of general-purpose AI systems, with the intent of making such systems safer and more reliable when they are developed."^[\[1\]](#fnk8qd5edrw4)^

History
-------

MIRI was founded in 2000 as the **Singularity Institute for Artificial Intelligence** by Brian Atkins, Sabine Atkins and [[Eliezer Yudkowsky]].^[\[2\]](#fno6zyxjrcfeq)^ It adopted its current name in 2013.^[\[3\]](#fncoahb85fxlm)^

Funding
-------

As of July 2022, MIRI has received over $14.7 million in funding from [[Open Philanthropy]],^[\[4\]](#fn8w6v9df3nov)^ nearly $900,000 from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund,^[\[5\]](#fnraxts7feeu)^^[\[6\]](#fnuep3xxzvezk)^ and over $670,000 from [[Effective Altruism Funds]].^[\[7\]](#fnui4lqgapu7p)^^[\[8\]](#fn3l7g23x5ugg)^^[\[9\]](#fn57iu5k62m1e)^^[\[10\]](#fn2bu39a9ez43)^
