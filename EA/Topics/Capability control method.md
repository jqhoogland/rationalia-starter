# Capability control method

A **capability control method** is a method that attempt to prevent undesirable outcomes from [[Artificial intelligence|artificial intelligence]] by restricting what the AI can do. Capability control methods encompass [[AI boxing]], incentive methods (including [[Anthropic capture|anthropic capture]]), stunting, and tripwires.^[\[1\]](#fnl1swhof7rb)^Â Capability control methods may be contrasted with [[Motivation selection method|motivation selection methods]], which attempt instead to restrict what the AI wants to do.
