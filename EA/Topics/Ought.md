# Ought

**Ought** is a research lab that develops mechanisms for delegating open-ended thinking to advanced machine learning systems.

Funding
-------

As of July 2022, Ought has received $5 million in funding from the [[Future Fund]],^[\[1\]](#fnl1lzvmsb9e)^ over $3.1 million from [[Open Philanthropy]],^[\[2\]](#fnem9a5l3zfsh)^ nearly $800,000 in funding from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund,^[\[3\]](#fnvecsuf3mh58)^^[\[4\]](#fnhmtg486slys)^ and $60,000 from [[Effective Altruism Funds]].^[\[5\]](#fntv8ffs8y0h)^^[\[6\]](#fne016fv5yctq)^
