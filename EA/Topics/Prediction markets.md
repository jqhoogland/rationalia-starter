# Prediction markets

**Prediction markets** (also known as **information markets** and **idea futures**) are markets for trading contracts about future events.

Further reading
---------------

Hanson, Robin (1995) [Could gambling save science? Encouraging an honest consensus](https://doi.org/10.1080/02691729508578768), *Social Epistemology*, vol. 9, pp. 3–33.
