# Effective Altruism Forum

The **Effective Altruism Forum** (often spelled **EA Forum**) is a community blog and forum focused on discussion of issues related to [[Effective altruism|effective altruism]]. It is supported by the [[Centre for Effective Altruism]].

History
-------

The EA Forum was launched in 2014, with seed content from a group blog that ran between 2012 and 2014, and published posts by early members of the effective altruism community.^[\[1\]](#fn4qjb2u1mex8)^^[\[2\]](#fnb78pdczee3m)^Â It was relaunched in 2018 with a new codebase and a dedicated team.^[\[3\]](#fn3bdj2ptwc6q)^
