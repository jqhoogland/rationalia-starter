# Simon Institute for Longterm Governance

The **Simon Institute for Longterm Governance** (**SI**) is a Swiss nonprofit organization that focuses on building the capacity of policy networks to mitigate [[Global catastrophic risk|global catastrophic risks]]. SI provides training programs and advice to policymakers, conducts research on long-term policy, and coordinates a network of longtermist actors and researchers. SI was founded in 2021.^[\[1\]](#fnba5911lx0ai)^

Funding
-------

As of July 2022, SI has received $820,000 in funding from the [[Future Fund]],^[\[2\]](#fnl5lcmoth3)^ and over $380,000 from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund.^[\[3\]](#fnn87e2i1ofg)^
