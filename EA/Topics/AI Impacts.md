# AI Impacts

**AI Impacts** is a nonprofit organization that researches answers to decision-relevant questions about the future of [[Artificial intelligence|artificial intelligence]]. It was founded in 2014 by [[Paul Christiano]] and Katja Grace.^[\[1\]](#fnj7tu0r1dr5r)^^[\[2\]](#fnm87ttm2zkx)^

Funding 
--------

As of July 2022, AI Impacts has received over $450,000 in funding from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund,^[\[3\]](#fnxzyd8918vs)^^[\[4\]](#fnr6hp12uxy1h)^^[\[5\]](#fny87npnoddnj)^ $250,000 from the [[Future Fund]],^[\[6\]](#fn6qtoi3x9q7d)^ over $180,000 from [[Open Philanthropy]]^[\[7\]](#fn1bv5jpvlys1)^, and $75,000 from [[Effective Altruism Funds]].^[\[8\]](#fng87m1t61v5n)^
