# ALLFED

**ALLFED** (the **Alliance to Feed the Earth in Disasters**) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to ensuring that people have enough to eat in the event of a [[Global catastrophic risk|global catastrophe]].

Funding
-------

As of June 2022, ALLFED has received nearly $1.3 million in funding from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund.^[\[1\]](#fnvgadg64qolj)^^[\[2\]](#fn9hxx1ql1z69)^^[\[3\]](#fntvtrs0ffpv)^
