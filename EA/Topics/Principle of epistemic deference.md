# Principle of epistemic deference

The **principle of epistemic deference** is the heuristic principle according to which, since the beliefs of a [[Superintelligence|superintelligence]] are more likely to be true than those of human beings, humanity should defer to it whenever feasible.

Further reading
---------------

Bostrom, Nick (2014) [*Superintelligence: Paths, Dangers, Strategies*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:BookSources/9780199678112), Oxford: Oxford University Press, p. 211.
