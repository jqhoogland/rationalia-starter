# Sentience Institute

**Sentience Institute** is a nonprofit think tank promoting interdisciplinary research on long-term social and technological change, particularly on [[Moral circle expansion|moral circle expansion]].

Funding
-------

As of July 2022, Sentience Institute has received $80,000 in funding from [[Effective Altruism Funds]],^[\[1\]](#fn8hla52a4ub3)^^[\[2\]](#fnh57yag81c7n)^ and $50,000 from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund.^[\[3\]](#fnr72t485s2y)^
