# Animal Advocacy Africa

**Animal Advocacy Africa** (**AAA**) is a program of Credence Institute that aims to develop the [[Effective animal advocacy|effective animal advocacy]] movement in Africa.

Funding
-------

As of June 2022, AAA has received over $90,000 in funding from [[Effective Altruism Funds]].^[\[1\]](#fnh2nouyc7n9w)^^[\[2\]](#fnzac57yzb3w)^
