# Motivation selection method

A **motivation selection method** is method that attempts to prevent undesirable outcomes from advanced [[Artificial intelligence|artificial intelligence]] by influencing what the AI wants to do. Motivation selection methods encompass direct specification, domesticity, [[Indirect normativity|indirect normativity]], and augmentation.^[\[1\]](#fn2mk6eej72fc)^Â Motivation selection methods may be contrasted with [[Capability control method|capability control methods]], which attempt instead to restrict what the AI can do.
