# Association for Long Term Existence and Resilience

**Association for Long Term Existence and Resilience** (**ALTER**) is an academic research and advocacy organization based in Israel focused on improving the short- and long-term future.

Funding
-------

As of July 2022, ALTER has received $400,000 in funding from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund,^[\[1\]](#fnhya3b9k032j)^ $320,000 from the [[Future Fund]],^[\[2\]](#fn4pq18hl02df)^ and over $150,000 from [[Effective Altruism Funds]].^[\[3\]](#fn8t254i9td85)^^[\[4\]](#fnayx2ri28lmf)^^[\[5\]](#fnf6224ek5rj5)^^[\[6\]](#fn172j5wj4wxvi)^
