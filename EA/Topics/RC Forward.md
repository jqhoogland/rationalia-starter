# RC Forward

**RC Forward** is an effective giving platform based in Canada that regrants to high-impact charities globally. It offers donors tax-efficient giving options for a wide range of high-impact charities across several cause areas, including [[Global health and development|global health]], [[Animal welfare|animal welfare]], [[Climate change|climate change]], and the [[Long-term future|long-term future]].
