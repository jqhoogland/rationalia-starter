# Hellish existential catastrophe

A **hellish existential catastrophe** (sometimes called a **hyper existential catastrophe**^[\[1\]](#fngfccwew1ugh)^^[\[2\]](#fngbwrpz88aa)^) is a catastrophe that not only [[Existential catastrophe|destroys humanity's long-term potential]] but also [[S-risk|creates negative value on an astronomical scale]].

Althaus, David & Lukas Gloor (2019) [Reducing risks of astronomical suffering: a neglected priority](https://longtermrisk.org/reducing-risks-of-astronomical-suffering-a-neglected-priority/), *Center on Long-Term Risk*, August.

Ord, Toby (2020) [*The Precipice: Existential Risk and the Future of Humanity*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:BookSources/1526600218), London: Bloomsbury Publishing, ch. 6, fn. 14.
