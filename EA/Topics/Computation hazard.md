# Computation hazard

A **computation hazard** (also known as a **metacomputational hazard**^[\[1\]](#fnwz4k60pviyl)^^[\[2\]](#fn0kmu0i3kxrfl)^) is a risk arising from great computational power.^[\[3\]](#fnuhjk5pnx0vn)^
