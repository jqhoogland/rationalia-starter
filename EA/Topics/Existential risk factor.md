# Existential risk factor

An **existential risk factor** is a factor that increases the probability of an [[Existential catastrophe|existential catastrophe]]. Conversely, an **existential security factor** is a factor that *decreases* the probability of an existential catastrophe.^[\[1\]](#fngmkob0nvxb)^ Analogous concepts have been used to analyze risks of [[Human extinction|human extinction]]^[\[2\]](#fnmrwsn1edcs8)^ and [[S-risk|s-risks]].^[\[3\]](#fnc9w4v3lzc5v)^
