# Quantified Uncertainty Research Institute

The **Quantified Uncertainty Research Institute** (**QURI**) is a nonprofit set up to study [[Forecasting|forecasting]] and epistemics. QURI was founded in 2019.

Funding
-------

As of June 2022, QURI has received over $650,000 in funding from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund,^[\[1\]](#fnc0d2u9mfrd)^^[\[2\]](#fnjt827nwjnlq)^ and $200,000 from the [[Future Fund]].^[\[3\]](#fn3k73fwbo6ot)^
