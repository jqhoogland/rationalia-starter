# Stuart Russell

**Stuart Jonathan Russell** (born 1962) is an English computer scientist, currently Professor of Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley. Russell is the author of [[Human Compatible|*Human Compatible: Artificial Intelligence and the Problem of Control*]] and the co-author of *Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach*.
