# Windfall Clause

The **Windfall Clause** is a proposed mechanism by which AI firms could commit to donate a significant amount of any eventual extremely large profits. The idea was first proposed by [[Nick Bostrom]],^[\[1\]](#fn5eepeewx6al)^ and subsequently developed  by the [[Centre for the Governance of AI]].
