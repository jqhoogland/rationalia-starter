# Centre for the Study of Existential Risk

The **Centre for the Study of Existential Risk** (**CSER**) is a research center at the University of Cambridge dedicated to the study and mitigation of [[EA/Topics/Existential risk|existential risks]].

History
-------

CSER was founded in 2012 by Huw Price, [[Jaan Tallinn]], and Lord Martin Rees.^[\[1\]](#fnv2ky8ntba1)^

Funding
-------

As of June 2022, CSER has received over $200,000 in funding from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund.^[\[2\]](#fnyzvnc9qxwj)^^[\[3\]](#fnj97l9i0x8r)^
