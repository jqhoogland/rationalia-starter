# Unprecedented risks

**Unprecedented risks** are risks which have never occurred. Such risks pose a number of unique challenges. Importantly, [[EA/Topics/Existential risk|existential risks]] are necessarily unprecedented.

Further reading
---------------

Ord, Toby (2020) [*The Precipice: Existential Risk and the Future of Humanity*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:BookSources/1-5266-0021-8), London: Bloomsbury Publishing, ch. 7.
