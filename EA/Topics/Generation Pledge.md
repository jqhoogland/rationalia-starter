# Generation Pledge

**Generation Pledge** is a nonprofit that encourages inheritors of large fortunes to [[Donation pledge|pledge to donate]] at least ten percent of their inheritance to [[Effective giving|effective charities]].

Funding
-------

As of July 2022, Generation Pledge has received nearly $400,000 in funding from the [[Survival and Flourishing]] Fund,^[\[1\]](#fn22qzyb7lwds)^^[\[2\]](#fn08kr0oozj2cr)^^[\[3\]](#fnkypdqxrfokn)^ and $30,000 from [[Effective Altruism Funds]].^[\[4\]](#fnso7r2st949i)^
