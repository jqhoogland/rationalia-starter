# Intellectual Progress via LessWrong
One of the core goals of the [LessWrong 2.0 platform](https://www.lesswrong.com/about) is to generate [[Intellectual Progress|intellectual progress]], specifically [[Differential Intellectual Progress|differential intellectual progress]] on important questions. There is much to discuss: should LessWrong 2.0 be a vehicle for intellectual progress? Is it succeeding?
