# Reversed Stupidity Is Not Intelligence
$P(\text{cults}|\text{aliens})$ isn’t less than $P(\text{cults}|\lnot\text{aliens})$.

> “The world’s greatest fool may say the Sun is shining, but that doesn’t make it dark out.”

— Robert Pirsig
