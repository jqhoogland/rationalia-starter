# Intuition

**Intuition** is the ability to acquire knowledge without recourse to conscious reasoning. Also relevant are **Intuition Pumps**, a thought experiment, a model, or anything else, that's structured to allow the thinker to use their intuition to develop an answer to a problem.Â
