A free-floating belief is a [[Belief|belief]] that both [doesn't follow from observations](https://www.lesswrong.com/tag/beliefs-require-observations) and doesn't restrict [[Making Beliefs Pay Rent|which experiences to anticipate]]. It is both unfounded and useless.
