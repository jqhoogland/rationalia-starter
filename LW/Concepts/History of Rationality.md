A post is relevant to the **history of rationality** if it discusses past events pertaining to [[Community|the LW community]] or another topic related to [rationality](https://www.lesswrong.com/tag/rationality), or the post is of strong historical interest.

You can see a list of all LW posts, from oldest to newest, [here](https://wiki.lesswrong.com/wiki/Less_Wrong/All_articles).

If you're trying to get a better understanding of the community and culture here, you may also be interested in the tag [Terminology/Jargon (meta)](https://www.lesswrong.com/tag/terminology-jargon-meta).

**Related Pages:** [[History of Less Wrong|History of Less Wrong]], [History](https://www.lesswrong.com/tag/history)
