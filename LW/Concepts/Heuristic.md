# Heuristic

A **heuristic** is a quick, intuitive strategy for reasoning or decision making, as opposed to more formal methods. Heuristics require much less time and energy to use, but sometimes go awry, producing [[Bias|bias]]. Common heuristics include:

- [[Absurdity Heuristic|Absurdity heuristic]] (`= [[Absurdity Heuristic|Absurdity heuristic]].status`)
- [[Affect Heuristic|Affect heuristic]] (`= [[Affect Heuristic|Affect heuristic]].status`)
- [[Availability Heuristic|Availability heuristic]] (`= [[Availability Heuristic|Availability heuristic]].status`)
- [[Representativeness Heuristic|Representativeness heuristic]] (`= [[Representativeness Heuristic|Representativeness heuristic]].status`)
- [[Contagion Heuristic|Contagion heuristic]] (`= [[Contagion Heuristic|Contagion heuristic]].status`)
- [[Peak-End Rule|Peak-end rule]] (`= [[Peak-End Rule|Peak-end rule]].status`)
