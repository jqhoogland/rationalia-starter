# Industrial Revolution
The Industrial Revolution was a set of economic and social changes that occurred in Europe and the United States in the 18th and 19th centuries, characterised by a transition from an "agrarian and handicraft economy to one dominated by industry and machine manufacturing" [[1](https://www.britannica.com/event/Industrial-Revolution)].Â
